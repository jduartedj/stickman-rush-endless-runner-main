Privacy Policy for Stickman Rush

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Introduction

Stickman Rush ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile game application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

High Scores: We store your game high scores locally on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device.

Automatically Collected Information

We do not collect device information or usage analytics directly. Any device information collected is done solely through third-party advertising services (see below).

Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. AdMob may collect and process data according to Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data
  • Location data (if permitted)

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game functionality
  • Display advertisements via Google AdMob

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob's use of advertising identifiers and data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

Data Retention

  • Local Data: High scores and game progress are stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
  • Advertising Data: Retention is controlled by Google AdMob according to their policies.

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to have it removed.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@stickmanrunner.com

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR including: right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, and objection to processing. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
