By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • App Preferences: Your settings such as which apps to block, schedule preferences, and whether you have purchased the Pro version.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including when Drinking Mode is activated or deactivated.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for the purpose of providing and improving the service.
  • Advertising Data: If you use the free version, our advertising partners may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads.

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • We do NOT read, access, or store the content of your messages.
  • We do NOT access your contacts or call history.
  • We do NOT track your location.
  • We do NOT collect any personal information such as your name, email, or phone number unless you contact us directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Process in-app purchases (Pro version)
  • Display advertisements (free version only)
  • Improve and optimize the App
  • Detect and prevent technical issues

3. Accessibility Service

The App uses Android's Accessibility Service to detect when you open a blocked app. This is essential for the core functionality of blocking apps while Drinking Mode is active.

  • The Accessibility Service ONLY detects which app is in the foreground.
  • It does NOT read any text, messages, or content from other apps.
  • It does NOT capture screenshots or record your screen.
  • It does NOT access your personal data in any way.
  • This data is processed locally on your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements in the free version. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing: For processing Pro version purchases. Google Privacy Policy

Pro users do not see ads, and therefore AdMob does not collect data from Pro users.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences.
  • We do NOT maintain servers that store your personal data.
  • Purchase information is handled securely by Google Play.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is NOT intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we process about you.
  • Delete: Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data.
  • Opt-out of personalized ads: Adjust your device's advertising settings or upgrade to Pro.
  • Disable Accessibility Service: You can disable the service at any time through Android Settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
